HISTORY



Aducktive Spicy Salted Egg Cornflakes was established on 2019 by Mr. Mohamad Anif Azami at Sungai Buloh, Selangor which is the location of Aducktive's Headquarters. This business have started from a small business to the successful growth business that make everyone know about its existence. In 2019, Mr. Anif Azami also hired more than 1000 stockiest and agents from Malaysia, Singapore and Brunei and still growing until now. As for the information, this product actually is a homemade product that can fit the taste of Malaysian and reach their target customer which is suitable for all ages except the baby. As the picture above, it is their first looks of Aducktive Spicy Salted Egg Cornflakes in a jar and now have change it packaging and sticker to more attractive and can attract the customer's interest. It is such a perfect snack to eat while doing work or watching a movie. Mostly the customer that they have are from students and workers.



Now let us know about the founder of this addictive snack. Name given is Mohamad Anif Azami. He was a foodpreneur that really successful in this food business. He was from Johor and started his business when he is 28 years old and finished his studied in bachelor's degree in material measurement. Actually, he have received so many job offer as from a public sector but he refused because he want to have his own business and find the money by himself. As for the beginning, he started venture into the business when he started in selling the 'kuih raya' at bazar from 7pm to 2am everyday and started in collecting the money for his marriage. When he already married to his wife, both of them started thinking an idea in selling something and the sources was from the kitchen when they saw a box of cornflakes cereal. So from that, they try to add the seasoning to make it delicious and started in designing their logo and brand name. As we know, the logo is a duck and the brand name also similar as duck. The brand name itself actually refer to addictive which means can make people want to eat more and more and the salted egg flavour is refer to the duck's egg.
